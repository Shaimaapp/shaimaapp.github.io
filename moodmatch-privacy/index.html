MoodMatch – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

1. Introduction

MoodMatch (“the App”, “we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our application.

By using MoodMatch, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Photos you upload
  • Profile details (username, bio)
  • Mood posts, reactions, and comments
  • Support messages

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • App usage analytics
  • Crash logs
  • Approximate location (city-level)
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID)

2.3 Third-Party Services

The App uses:

  • Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Storage, Crashlytics, Analytics)
  • Google AdMob / Google Ads SDK for ad display
  • AI Image Processing services for filters and enhancement (if used)

We do not sell any personal data.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide core app features
  • Improve app performance
  • Personalize user experience
  • Display relevant ads
  • Process images using AI (if used)
  • Prevent abuse and suspicious activity

4. Data Sharing

We may share limited data with:

  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics providers
  • AI processing tools (only when user activates the feature)

Data is only shared with explicit user consent as required by Apple Guideline 5.1.2(i).

5. Age & Eligibility

MoodMatch is intended for users who meet the age requirement of the Apple App Store. By using the App, users confirm that they meet the minimum eligible age in their region.

If we discover data belonging to an underage user, it will be deleted immediately.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Delete your account
  • Request removal of your data
  • Edit your profile information
  • Withdraw your consent at any time

To request data deletion, email us at: co.87s@hotmail.com

7. Data Security

We use encryption and security measures to protect user data. While no system is 100% secure, we take all reasonable steps to safeguard information.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use of the App means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

For questions or requests, contact: co.87s@hotmail.com
